# Rihanna Razavi

### PhD Candidate | Quantitative Psychology & Human Development | University of Notre Dame

---

I work at the intersection of **computational social science**, **behavioral measurement**, and **machine learning** — using large-scale data to understand human development, adolescent health, and family dynamics.

🔬 **Current Research:** Cluster analysis of adolescent mental health & substance use patterns (NSDUH 2024) · Longitudinal family dynamics using latent growth curve models · Generative agent simulations of child development  
🎓 **Education:** PhD Candidate, University of Notre Dame · M.S. Applied Statistics · M.S. Computer Science · B.S. Sharif University of Technology, Tehran  
🏛️ **Industry Background:** Production Specialist, [Fidibo](https://fidibo.com/) — Iran's leading digital reading platform
